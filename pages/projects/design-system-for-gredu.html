Design System for Gredu (EdTech Platform)

CLIENT

Gredu

MY ROLE

  • UI/UX Designer
  • Design System Team

DURATION

4 months

YEAR

2020

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Gredu is an EdTech Startup that focused on integrating the school environment for teachers, parents, and students.
At that time, we have several products handled by several different designers.
In this project, I get in charge of build initial design system along with Project Lead.

MAIN GOAL

Get everything aligned and has a consistent design quality in all product even it handled by different designers.

TOOLS

Sketch App, Abstract, InVision, Jira, Trello, and Miro.
