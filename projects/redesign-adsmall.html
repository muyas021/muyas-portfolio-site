Redesign AdsMall (Advertising Marketplace)

CLIENT

AdsMall

MY ROLE

  • User Research
  • Visual Design
  • Interaction Design

DURATION

4 months and freeze

YEAR

2020

PROJECT OVERVIEW

AdsMall is an Advertising Startup in Indonesia that focused on connecting advertiser and advertising media publisher.
Before I joined the team, there is no designer involved in product development.
In this project, I worked closely with the Product Owner and 3 Engineers.

MAIN GOAL

Platform redesign and brand redesign as our commitment to OKR.

TOOLS

Figma, Trello, Confluence, and Google Sheets.

Before the redesign

The product that released by the predecessor

Before going too far, I took a look at the product that has released by the predecessor. The purpose is to know about the current condition and gained some data for the next step—interview with the predecessor.

Interview with the predecessor

I conducted an interview with the predecessor to know more about “things that have done well” and “things that need to improve”.

Here are some insights I got from the interview with the predecessor (CTO, VP, Engineer, Marketing, and Operation):

  • There are no designers to make sure the design process;
  • There's too much thing to cover on the previous;
  • The poor landing page makes the user often didn't go with the product;
  • Hard to place the logo in any media since the logo has too much color and a poor contrast;
  • The logo not get so much trust in front of the client.

Skip the usability test for the current product

Our current product is not ready for a usability test since we change our MVP. So, we choose to do a user interview instead.

Refine the MVP

User interview

In the first round, we focused on interviewing advertiser to know their behaviour and know about advertising media that they spend the most.

Design mandates to cover in MVP

After gained the data from user interview, we (me, Product Owner, and VP) discussed things to cover in MVP by matching the OKR and insights from the interview.

How the MVP Product going

Until I have written this case study, the MVP Product has been going halfway. The project has been freeze because the company can't survive. So, here's the showcase of the redesign version that had been done.

Learnings

  1. When you first involved in a project that has done before, try to learn as many things from the predecessor. It helps you understand the background and prevent you from judging the bad legacy only.
  2. The project may have not going as expected. However, there come a lesson for me to be better in future.
